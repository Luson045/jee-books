PHYSICS

physics, being the most scoring subject needs formula and knowledge in mains and deep knowledge and practical thinking in advance is one of the most focused subject

    1)CONCEPT OF PHYSICS BY HC VERMA

    This book is highly recommended for JEE aspirants and is considered a must-read for building a strong foundation in physics.

    2)DC PANDEY SERIES

    This book is well known for its clear and concise explanations of the concepts and its comprehensive coverage of the JEE syllabus.

    3)PHYSICS GALAXY ADVANCE ILLUSTRATION

    "Advanced Illustration in Physics" by Ashish Arora is a well-known book among JEE aspirants, and it is known to have a good collection of challenging problems with detailed solutions.If you feel that you need more practice with advanced-level problems and want to challenge yourself beyond the standard JEE syllabus, then "Advanced Illustration in Physics" could be a good choice for you. However, if you are just starting with JEE preparation or feel that you need to strengthen your foundational concepts, it may be better to start with other books that cover the basics first.

MATHEMATICS

This subject gives goosebump to a lot of students, but with good preparation one can achieve most amazing marks in maths.Here are a few books recommended

    1)Mathematics for Class 11 and 12 by R.D. SHARMA

    This is a popular book among JEE aspirants and covers all the essential topics in mathematics for Classes 11 and 12. It contains a large number of solved examples and practice problems to help students build a strong foundation in the subject.

    2)PLAYING WITH GRAPHS

    Playing with Graphs is a popular book for mathematics students, especially those who are preparing for competitive exams like JEE. It is written by Amit M Agarwal, who is a renowned mathematician and teacher with years of experience in coaching students for JEE and other exams. The book covers all the essential topics in graphs, such as types of graphs, equations of lines, distance and section formula, circles, parabolas, ellipses, and hyperbolas. The book is structured in a way that makes it easy for students to understand the concepts and apply them to solve problems. The explanations are clear and concise, and the book contains numerous solved examples and practice problems to help students reinforce their understanding. Playing with Graphs is a useful resource for JEE aspirants as it covers a significant portion of the mathematics syllabus and provides students with a solid foundation in the subject. The book is also useful for those who want to develop their problem-solving skills and improve their ability to think logically and analytically. Overall, Playing with Graphs is a well-written and comprehensive book that is highly recommended for mathematics students who are preparing for competitive exams like JEE

    3)ADVANCE PROBLEMS IN MATHEMATICS BY VK MEHTA

    "Advanced Problems in Mathematics" by VK Sir is a popular book for JEE preparation, specifically for the mathematics section. It is written by V.K. Mehta, who is a renowned mathematics teacher with years of experience in coaching students for JEE and other competitive exams. The book covers all the essential topics in mathematics for JEE, such as algebra, trigonometry, geometry, calculus, and vectors. It contains a large number of challenging problems with varying levels of difficulty, designed to help students develop their problem-solving skills and master the concepts. The book is known for its unique approach to problem-solving, which involves breaking down complex problems into simpler parts and using step-by-step methods to arrive at the correct solution. The explanations are clear and concise, and the book contains numerous solved examples and practice problems to help students reinforce their understanding. Advanced Problems in Mathematics by VK Sir is a useful resource for JEE aspirants who are looking to improve their mathematics skills and achieve success in the exam. It is recommended for students who have a strong foundation in mathematics and are looking for more challenging problems to solve
